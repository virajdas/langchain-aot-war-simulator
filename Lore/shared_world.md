## Global Political Balance
Marley is the largest Titan-power-backed colonial empire controlling multiple vassal states. Other nations resent Titan monopolization and modernize militaries to counter Titans. Paradis is internationally feared as the symbolic legacy of Eldian domination.

## Eldians
Eldians possess Titan transformation potential. Global law treats them as bio-weapons. Segregation policies vary by state.

## Titan Warfare
Nine Titans define battlefield superiority via unmatched mobility and destructive capacity. Anti-Titan cannons, armored vehicles, aerial bombing, and rail weapons increasingly threaten Titan dominance.

## Marley Warrior Program
Eldian youths trained to inherit Titan powers. Serve as frontline assault weapons and imperial propaganda symbols.

## Paradis Geography
Isolated island nation formerly enclosed by massive wall structures. Rapid modernization occurred following foreign incursions.

## International Doctrine
World powers seek Titan deterrence while fearing rogue Titan deployment. Coalitions form to prevent Paradis sovereignty and eliminate Founding Titan capability.
