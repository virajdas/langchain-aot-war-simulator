# Marley Internal Intelligence & Strategy

## National Doctrine
Hegemony maintenance through Titan terror, alliances, and international narrative engineering.

## Political Characteristics
Marley assemblies dominated by military-industrial interests. Civilian councils exist but remain subordinate.

## Eldian Policy
Eldians imprisoned within internment zones. Warriors viewed simultaneously as liabilities and strategic assets.

## Military Structure

Titan Warriors:
- Jaw: Urban breach and assassination
- Armor: Heavy assault and frontline spearbreak
- Cart: Logistics and anti-Titan artillery deployment
- Beast: Long-range devastation and troop coordination
- Female: Tactical adaptability

Conventional Forces:
- Mass artillery deployment
- Naval Titan carriers
- Air experimentation

## Intelligence Operations
Infiltration teams dispatched to Paradis prior to major invasions. Global diplomatic misinformation campaigns to sustain anti-Eldian sentiment.

## Political Fears
Titan superiority eroding due to technology. Internal pressure rising against continued Eldian exploitation.

## Strategic Goals
- Secure Founding Titan.
- Keep world alliance coalition intact.
- Preserve global legitimacy while suppressing humanitarian scrutiny.
