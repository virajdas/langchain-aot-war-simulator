# Paradis Intelligence & Factional Overview

## Governance Model
Semi-militarized republic with fractured legislative body.

## Military Factions

Scouts:
- Mobile reconnaissance and offensive Titan engagement.
- Early adopters of experimental anti-Titan tech.
- Ideologically driven by exploration and freedom.

Garrison:
- Defensive corps responsible for wall fortifications, artillery installations, city defense, and evacuation tactics.

Military Police:
- Internal security force.
- Maintains political order.
- Historically aligned with ruling elite.

## Political Groups

Diplomatic Reformists:
- Seek international recognition and economic normalization.

Militant Expansionists:
- Argue only Titan deterrence ensures survival.

Moderates:
- Seek delay-equilibrium strategies.

## Intelligence Network
Dependent on defectors, maritime scouting, captured intelligence.

## Titan Forces
- Founding Titan: Planet-scale biological weapon authority.
- Secondary Titan inheritance among Scout commanders historically.

## Public Morale
Civilian population traumatized by isolationism breakdown and genocidal threat narratives.

## Ethical Constraints
Ongoing civil discourse over Titan weaponization legality and existential moral cost.
